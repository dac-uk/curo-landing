Frequently Asked Questions

Curo uses Open Banking to connect to your bank securely. Here's how it works:

  1. Open the Curo app and go to Accounts in the main menu
  2. Tap Add Account and search for your bank
  3. You'll be redirected to your bank's secure authorisation page — this is your bank's own login, not Curo's
  4. Log in and approve the connection. You'll be asked to grant read-only access to your account data
  5. Once authorised, you'll be returned to Curo and your transactions will begin syncing

Curo has read-only access — we can never see your password, move money, or make any changes to your account.

Bank connections expire after 90 days as required by Open Banking regulations. Curo will prompt you to re-authorise before your connection expires.

Your Curo Pro subscription is managed through the Apple App Store. To cancel:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions
  3. Find Curo Pro in the list and tap it
  4. Tap Cancel Subscription and confirm

You'll retain access to Curo Pro features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will revert to the Free tier — your data and connected accounts are preserved.

If you're having trouble cancelling, you can also contact Apple Support directly at support.apple.com.

You can delete your Curo account and all associated data at any time:

  1. Open the Curo app and go to Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  3. Read the confirmation and tap Delete my account and data

This will permanently delete your Curo account, all your transaction data, and all connected bank connections. This action cannot be undone.

Important: Deleting your Curo account does not cancel your App Store subscription. Please cancel your Curo Pro subscription in the App Store first (see above) before deleting your account.

If you'd prefer, you can also email us at hello@bamboofutures.co.uk and we will delete your account and data manually within 5 business days. This is your right under UK GDPR. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

There are a few reasons your bank connection might stop syncing:

  1. Connection expired — Open Banking connections expire every 90 days. Curo will prompt you to re-authorise. Simply tap the notification and follow the steps to reconnect
  2. Changed bank credentials — if you've updated your banking password or security settings, you may need to re-authorise the connection
  3. Bank maintenance — occasionally your bank's API may be temporarily unavailable. This usually resolves within a few hours

To reconnect, go to Accounts in the app and tap the bank connection showing an error. Then follow the re-authorisation steps.

If the issue persists, email us at hello@bamboofutures.co.uk with the name of your bank and we'll look into it.

Curo is built on Open Banking, which is regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). Your bank connection grants Curo read-only access only. That means:

  1. We can see your transactions and balances — nothing else
  2. We cannot move money, make payments, or change anything on your account
  3. We never see your banking password
  4. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  5. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers

For full details on how we handle your data, please read our Privacy Policy.

Under UK GDPR you have the right to receive a copy of all personal data we hold about you (a Subject Access Request).

To make a request, email us at hello@bamboofutures.co.uk with the subject line "Subject Access Request" and include the email address associated with your Curo account.

We will respond within one calendar month with a copy of your data in a portable format. We may ask you to verify your identity before fulfilling the request.
