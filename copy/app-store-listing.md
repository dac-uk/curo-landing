## Description

Curo is an AI-powered personal finance assistant that turns your bank transactions into clarity. No spreadsheets, no manual categorisation, no guessing where your money went — just open the app and understand your finances in seconds.

**MORNING BRIEFING**
Start every day with a concise, personalised financial summary. Curo reviews yesterday's spending, highlights upcoming bills, and flags anything unusual — all before your first coffee. Think of it as a financial newspaper written just for you.

**SMART BILL TRACKING**
Curo automatically detects recurring payments across all your accounts. It tracks price changes over time, warns you before bills hit, and helps you spot subscriptions you've forgotten about. Never be caught off guard by a payment again.

**ANOMALY DETECTION**
Unusual charge at 3am? Unexpected spending spike? Curo's AI continuously monitors your transactions and alerts you to anything that doesn't look right. Catch fraud, billing errors, and accidental purchases before they become problems.

**ASK CURO — NATURAL LANGUAGE QUERIES**
"How much did I spend on food this month?" "What's my biggest recurring expense?" Just ask. Curo understands natural language and searches across all your connected accounts to give you instant, accurate answers. No more digging through transaction lists.

**PRIVACY BY DESIGN**
Your financial data is sensitive, and we treat it that way. Curo uses Open Banking standards (read-only access) and encrypts your data end-to-end. We never sell your data, never share it with advertisers, and never will. Bank-grade security isn't a feature — it's a requirement.

**CONNECT YOUR ACCOUNTS**
Link your UK bank accounts, credit cards, and savings accounts through secure Open Banking connections. Curo supports all major UK banks and building societies.

**PRICING**
• Free tier: 1 connected account, Morning Briefing, basic bill tracking
• Pro (£4.99/month): Unlimited accounts, anomaly detection, Ask Curo, full transaction history, smart bill insights, priority support

Curo is built in London by Bamboo Futures Ltd. We're a small team obsessed with making personal finance simple, private, and genuinely useful.
